ACCOMMODATIONS



Our wedding block is at the Sheraton Providence Hotel. The hotel provides a free shuttle from the Providence Airport, the Providence train station, and the Providence bus station. We will be providing transportation to and from the reception venue, so you should be able to stay the weekend without renting a car. Please reserve your rooms by May 31st to lock in our wedding rate. Given that this is the busiest weekend of the year in Rhode Island, we suggest you book your room as soon as possible to avoid being without a place to stay during the wedding. Call the Sheraton directly at 1-401-738-4000 and book under the Szajnberg-Martinez wedding.
