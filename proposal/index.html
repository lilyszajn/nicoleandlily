    How it All Went Down

    Coming up with a clever proposal is a lot easier when you have a 12 person acappella group at your disposal.

    Nicole and I met in the semi-professional acappella group, Treble in 2010. I knew from early on that if we ever were going to get engaged, I'd have to include Treble in the engagement, as it is not only the reason we met, but is also comprised of 10 women who are some of our closest friends. When Isaac's live lip-dub proposal went viral, I knew how things would have to go down.

    We told Nicole that Treble had been hired to sing for a proposal on the High Line in NYC. She learned her part, and showed up exactly when we told her to - about 30 minutes after the rest of us had arrived. Nervous that she'd arrived late as she heard us singing, she hurried over to the group, and then slowly realized it was actually for her. When I popped out at the end and dropped to one knee, she said yes and the entire group burst into tears.
